About

The modern beginnings of the University of Puget Sound Sailing team began sometime in 2006 when a group of energetic students began the process of bringing one of the greatest sports to a wonderful school in the Northwest. After several short stints and breaks, the team took on full form in the fall of 2008 and has continued to build momentum ever since.

From 4-6 interested sailors, the team has evolved with more than 15 committed yachts men and women who compete throughout the Northwest and South Western Canada.
